About NorthernEdge Legal Solutions

At NorthernEdge Legal Solutions, we are committed to providing exceptional legal services to our clients. With a focus on corporate, commercial, and intellectual property law, our team delivers strategic legal solutions that are tailored to your unique needs.

Tim J. Harmar, BA (Hons), JD, MBA, LLM, LLM, M.Eng., CAPP

Tim J. Harmar is the founder and principal lawyer at NorthernEdge Legal Solutions, bringing a wealth of experience in corporate, commercial, and intellectual property law to his clients. With a diverse and interdisciplinary background, Tim is uniquely positioned to offer comprehensive legal services that meet the complex needs of businesses and individuals alike.

Tim holds multiple advanced degrees, including a Master of Laws (LLM) in Business Law from York University’s Osgoode Hall Law School, a Master of Laws in Banking and Financial Services Law, and a Master of Engineering in Cybersecurity Policy and Compliance from George Washington University. Additionally, he holds an MBA with a specialization in Finance and Accounting, underscoring his ability to bridge the gap between legal expertise and business acumen.

Before founding NorthernEdge Legal Solutions, Tim served as an Associate at Wishart Law Firm LLP, where he gained significant experience in a broad range of legal areas including administrative law, civil litigation, employment law, environmental law, and more. His work often involved advising on complex transactions, drafting high-value commercial agreements, and navigating regulatory landscapes. Tim’s ability to combine sharp legal insight with a practical understanding of business operations has earned him a reputation as a trusted advisor.

Tim is also a Chartered Privacy and Access Professional (CAPP) through the Privacy and Access Council of Canada, reflecting his commitment to data privacy and information governance—an increasingly critical area in today's digital world.

Outside of his legal practice, Tim is deeply involved in the community. He has served on the Board of Directors for several organizations, including the Sault Ste. Marie Innovation Centre, where he also worked as a Business Development Manager. In this role, he was instrumental in supporting local startups and fostering innovation within the community.
